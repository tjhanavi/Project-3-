About the Artists

Misan Harriman

Misan Harriman is a renowned photographer and activist. His work has been instrumental in documenting social movements and capturing the human spirit. Known for his powerful images from Black Lives Matter protests, Misan's photography transcends boundaries and delivers poignant messages of justice, resilience, and hope. His ability to capture raw emotions makes him a distinguished artist in contemporary photography.

Domizia Salusest

Domizia Salusest is celebrated for her unique approach to portrait photography. Her work often explores the intricate connections between individuals and their environments. Domizia's portraits are known for their depth, bringing out the essence of her subjects with a blend of authenticity and artistry. Her contribution to the "100 Reasons to Rise" project highlights her talent for capturing the extraordinary in the everyday.
